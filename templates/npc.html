Adaptive NPC System

An autonomous system capable of analyzing the environment, learning player patterns and modifying its behavior in real time through reinforcement learning.

ARCHITECTURE

System Architecture

Perception

Reads environment variables: player position, threat level, available resources and world state.

Decision

Dynamic action selection using a learned policy. Evaluates multiple options and picks the one with the highest expected reward.

Memory

Stores past events in an experience buffer to adjust behavior and avoid repeating mistakes.

Reward

Evaluates outcomes through a reward function to optimize the agent's internal policy over time.

APPLICATIONS

Applications in Video Games

Urban Sandbox

NPCs that react to the player's reputation, crime in the area and dynamic world events. Every player action modifies collective NPC behavior.

Strategic Survival

Enemies that learn frequent player routes, anticipate ambushes and coordinate group attacks based on observed patterns.

Dynamic RPG

Secondary characters that remember past interactions, adjust their dialogue and switch factions based on player decisions.

Competitive FPS

Bots that analyze the opponent's playstyle and adapt their tactics: flanking, cover or frontal pressure based on match history.
