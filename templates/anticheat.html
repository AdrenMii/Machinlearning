USE CASE 4 · ANOMALY DETECTION

Anti-Cheat Detection

Machine Learning models that analyze player behavior patterns to detect cheating, bots and unfair advantages in competitive online games.

PROBLEM

Problem Description

Cheating is one of the biggest threats to competitive online gaming. Players use aimbots, wallhacks, speed hacks and other tools to gain unfair advantages, destroying the experience for honest players and damaging the game's reputation.

Traditional rule-based anti-cheat systems are easily bypassed once cheaters identify their detection patterns. ML-based systems learn what normal and abnormal behavior looks like, making them much harder to fool and able to detect new cheating methods automatically.

MACHINE LEARNING TYPE

How the Model Works

Type

Anomaly Detection (Unsupervised)

Input Data

Aim precision, reaction time, movement patterns, click frequency

Expected Output

Cheat probability score — flag or ban

Real-World Use

Valorant, CS2, PUBG, Fortnite

PROCESS

Detection Process

1. Behavioral Baseline

The model learns what normal human behavior looks like: typical aim patterns, reaction times and movement speeds across millions of players.

2. Real-Time Monitoring

Every match, the system records hundreds of data points per second: mouse movement, click timing, headshot percentage and position data.

3. Anomaly Scoring

The model compares the player's behavior against the baseline and assigns a cheat probability score. Values above a threshold trigger a review.

4. Action & Update

Flagged accounts are reviewed or banned. Confirmed cheater data is fed back into the model to improve future detections.

ANALYSIS

Advantages and Limitations

✅ Advantages

Detects unknown cheats

Learns patterns rather than signatures, so new cheats can still be caught.

Scales easily

Can monitor millions of players simultaneously without manual review.

Continuous improvement

Gets smarter over time as more data from confirmed cheaters is added.

❌ Limitations

False positives

Skilled legitimate players may be flagged due to unusually high performance.

Arms race

Cheat developers analyze the system and adapt their tools to avoid detection.

Privacy concerns

Deep behavioral monitoring raises questions about how player data is collected and stored.
